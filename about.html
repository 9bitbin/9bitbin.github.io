Hi, I’m Himal Shrestha 👋

I’m an IT professional, problem-solver, and writer based in New York — passionate about connecting technology with the human experience.

I’ve always believed that code and creativity share the same language — both are systems that reflect how we think, grow, and build meaning. Over time, my work in IT, writing, and digital design has evolved into something more holistic: a balance between clarity and creativity.

Rotating Soul is my ongoing project — part journal, part workshop. Every section of this site explores a different side of my life:

  • 🧰 Mind over Machine — my experiments in IT, automation, and tech tutorials
  • 🧠 Himal’s Realm — reflections on psychology, philosophy, and the quiet mind
  • 🌿 The Reflective Engineer — systems for growth, health, and discipline
  • 💬 Tech & Tranquility — community, connection, and how people shape progress
  • 🎨 Wired Soul Journal — art, lyrics, and digital emotion in motion

Beyond work, I’m curious about how technology can heal instead of hurry — how creativity can coexist with structure — and how small systems, both human and digital, can make life more peaceful.

If this sounds like your wavelength, feel free to explore the blog or reach out — everything here is built with a mix of curiosity, caffeine, and care.
